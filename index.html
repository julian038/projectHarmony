About Project Harmony

Learning to play an instrument takes patience, persistence, and focus — the same qualities students need to excel in school and in their future career. We support our students every step of the way, helping them achieve their goals throughout their childhood.

  
  • 2,000 Students from 345 Schools
  • 100 Teaching Artists
  • 39 Orchestras and Ensembles
  • Over 100 Square Miles Served Across Los Angeles
  • 100% of 52 Graduating Seniors were Accepted to College in 2015

Music Takes Commitment

Learning to play an instrument takes patience, persistence, and focus — the same qualities students need to excel in school and in their future career. We support our students every step of the way, helping them achieve their goals throughout their childhood.

Enroll
  • INSTRUMENTS
  • We give each student an instrument to take home
  • LESSONS
  • Five hours of instruction per week, taught by a professional teaching artist
  • ORCHESTRAS
  • Every student plays in a group — all instruments, ages & skill levels
  • CONCERTS
  • Students perform in concerts & community events throughout the year
  • PROGRESS CHECK-INS
  • Students & teachers meet one-on-one to make sure everyone is on track
  • FIELD TRIPS
  • We organize field trips to concerts & cultural events
  • FAMILY SUPPORT
  • We connect parents with supporting services
  • COLLEGE SCHOLARSHIPS
  • We award college scholarships to eligible graduates
